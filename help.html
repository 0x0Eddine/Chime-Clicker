Chime Clicker
About Help

Introduction

Chime Clicker is League of Legends themed incremental game based off Bard, the Wandering Caretaker. Your goal is to collect chimes, kill monsters, and gain experience as you level up to victory. To help you get there are a series of items, upgrades, and spells that you'll unlock along the way.


Chimes and Meeps

Click the large chime button in the top of the left panel to collect Chimes. After gathering enough, you'll earn Meeps. Each Meep grants bonus damage to help you kill monsters. Collecting chimes also grants experience to help you level up.


Monsters

Click the large monster button in the bottom of the left panel to deal damage to the current monster. After defeating a monster by bringing its health to zero, you'll be rewarded with gold (to buy items and upgrades) and experience (to level up). New monsters are unlocked at each level, but get progressively harder to kill. You can always go back and fight easier opponents if the latest monster proves too strong.


Items

In the center panel are items to help you gather more chimes and kill monsters faster. Each item has its own price that increases for every unit purchased. Buy multiple of an item at once using the additional purchase buttons. Item stats consist of the following:

  • Discovery - Chimes gathered per click, 1 discovery = 1 chime per click.
  • Swiftness - Automatic chime gathering, 1 swiftness = 1 chime click per second.
  • Power - Damage dealt per click, 1 power = 1 damage per click.
  • Agility - Automatic damage dealing, 1 agility = 1 damage click per second.
  • Income - Gold generation, 1 income = 1 gold per second.

When manually clicking the chime button, you'll gather additional chimes equal to 2% of total CPS (chimes per second, Discovery × Swiftness). Similarly, you'll deal extra damage equal to 2% of total DPS (damage per second, Power × Agility) when clicking the monster button.


Upgrades

After leveling up enough, you'll start to unlock upgrades for your items. As with items, each upgrade has its own price and stats. After purchasing an upgrade, all past and future units of the associated item are granted bonus stats.


Spells

In the final panel, you'll find a series of spells, each with its own unique effect. Unlock them all by leveling up or purchasing specific upgrades. Spells are free to use, but most have cooldowns, during which a given spell becomes unusable. Hover over unlocked spells to learn how each works.


Hotkeys

Most actions can be triggered via hotkeys. Here is a list of available hotkeys and their corresponding actions:

  • 1 - Buy item 1.
  • 2 - Buy item 2.
  • 3 - Buy item 3.
  • 4 - Buy item 4.
  • 5 - Buy item 5.
  • 6 - Buy item 6.
  • 7 - Buy item 7.

  • ctrl + # - Buy 10 units of item #.
  • shift + # - Buy 100 units of item #.
  • ctrl + shift + # - Buy 1000 units of item #.
  • alt + # - Buy upgrade for item #.

  • q - Activate spell 1.
  • w - Activate spell 2.
  • e - Activate spell 3.
  • r - Activate spell 4.
  • t - Activate spell 5.
  • y - Activate spell 6.

  • a - Previous monster.
  • s - Next monster.
  • d - Hide Meep progress.
  • f - Hide Monster progress.
